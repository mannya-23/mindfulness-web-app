About the Developers

Who We Are

For our CSSI final project, we decided to create Mindful Escape, a wellness & self-care Web App aimed to help individuals identify what is causing them stress and connect them to solutions. Mental health is an increasingly apparent problem and the goal of Mindful Escape is to provide you with advice, quotes, and a community that helps you feel supported. You are NEVER alone and if you ever need an escape from reality, Mindful Escape is there for you.
